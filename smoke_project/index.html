Against the threat of wildfire!

Tens of thousands wildfire break out across United States every year! Apart from their direct threat to human lives or properties, wildfire smoke can quickly spread across states and pollute the air that brings threat to millions of people even they are far away from the wildfire. Current wildfire surveillance systems rely on special sensors and human aid to indentify wildfire which is expensive and non-scalable. We bring the solution by machine learning.
